Onboarding

Start Using Bison Wallet in Minutes

Follow these simple steps to set up your wallet and begin your decentralized journey.

  1. 1

    Download & Install

    Choose the correct version for your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux) from the Download section and install the application.

  2. 2

    Create or Restore Wallet

    Launch Bison Wallet and follow the on-screen instructions to create a new wallet or restore an existing one using your seed phrase.

  3. 3

    Secure Your Seed Phrase

    Crucial Step: Write down your seed phrase accurately and store it securely offline. Never share it. This is the only way to recover your funds.

  4. 4

    Configure Assets & Connect

    Enable the assets you wish to manage and trade. Connect to the DCRDEX markets to view trading pairs.

  5. 5

    Understand Trading Bonds (DCRDEX)

    DCRDEX requires a small amount of DCR locked in a refundable on-chain bond to place orders, preventing spam. Understand this requirement before trading. More details in the Support/FAQ.

Your Security is Paramount

Bison Wallet is designed with security at its core, empowering you with control.

Self-Custodial

You, and only you, hold the private keys to your funds. Bison Wallet software never takes custody of your assets.

Private

No KYC or AML. No registration. No names. DCRDEX does not track IPs when you retrieve or relay order info.

Lightweight SPV Technology

Simplified Payment Verification offers a balance of security and speed without needing full blockchain downloads.

Your Responsibility

Safeguarding your seed phrase is vital. Store it securely offline. Loss of your seed phrase means loss of access to your funds.

Always verify software sources and be cautious of phishing attempts.

Transparent Fee Structure

Understand the costs associated with using Bison Wallet and DCRDEX.

DCRDEX Trading Fees: Zero

DCRDEX charges no trading fees for executing swaps. You only pay the standard blockchain network fees required for the atomic swap transactions themselves (e.g., Bitcoin network fees for BTC swaps, Decred network fees for DCR swaps).

Trading stablecoins on Polygon typically results in very low network fees.

Bison Wallet itself does not add any extra fees for standard wallet operations or trading.

  • On-chain voting empowers the community to direct the project's future.

Bison Wallet is a key component of the Decred ecosystem, providing seamless access to its features and the DCRDEX decentralized exchange.
